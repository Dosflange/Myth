This page describes the following instruction:

  • Group: PAIR
  • Mnemonic: ig
  • Opcode: 211d, D3h
  • Encoding: 1_101_0011b (PAIR_SRC_DST)
  • Pseudo-code: g=i; pc++
  • I:INNER G:GLOBAL

  • Source is the I register.
  • Destination is the G register.
