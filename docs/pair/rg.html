This page describes the following instruction:

  • Group: PAIR
  • Mnemonic: rg
  • Opcode: 195d, C3h
  • Encoding: 1_100_0011b (TRAP_SRC_DST)
  • Pseudo-code: g=r; pc++
  • R:RESULT G:GLOBAL

  • Source is the R register.
  • Destination is the G register.
