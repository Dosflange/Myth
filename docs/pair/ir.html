This page describes the following instruction:

  • Group: PAIR
  • Mnemonic: ir
  • Opcode: 212d, D4h
  • Encoding: 1_101_0100b (TRAP_SRC_DST)
  • Pseudo-code: r=i; pc++
  • I:INNER R:RESULT

  • Source is the I register.
  • Destination is the R register.
