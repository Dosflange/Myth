This page describes the following instruction:

  • Group: ALU
  • Mnemonic: IOR
  • Opcode: 25d, 19h
  • Encoding: 0001_1001b (ALU_OPERATION)
  • Pseudo-code: r=r|o; pc++

This instructions computes the bitwise inclusive OR of R and O and stores the result in R.
